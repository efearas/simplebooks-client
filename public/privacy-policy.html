Technologies such as cookies, beacons, tags and scripts are used by SimpleBooks. “Cookies” are small data files that contain a string of characters that are sent to your computer browser from our servers when our website is accessed. SimpleBooks uses cookies to recognize your browser, track your movement through the website, track traffic source and measure the success of a particular marketing campaign.
